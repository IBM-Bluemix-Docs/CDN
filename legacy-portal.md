# Legacy Customer Portal

Some legacy customers only have access to the Customer Portal through `https://control.softlayer.com/`. Follow these instructions to log in, then perform any of the activities as described in CDN documentation.

From your browser, open the [Customer Portal ![External link icon](../../icons/launch-glyph.svg "External link icon")](https://control.softlayer.com/){: new_window} and log into your account.
